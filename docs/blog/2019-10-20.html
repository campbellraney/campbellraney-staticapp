The Kraken Awakens

Arrr matey, the sea be restless today. The kraken has stirred from its slumber and left wreckage in its wake.

We set sail at dawn, unaware of the beast that lurked below. The sky turned black as ink and the waters boiled. First the mast snapped, then the crew panicked. It was chaos.

But we lived to tell the tale. Barely. And the legend of the kraken grows ever larger.
